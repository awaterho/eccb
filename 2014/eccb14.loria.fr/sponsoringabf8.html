Why you should sponsor ECCB

The ECCB'14 Sponsorship Program provides strategic opportunities for companies and other organizations to make an impact at the ECCB'14 Annual Conference. Sponsoring ECCB can help you maximize corporate visibility, reach leading scientists, showcase new products and services, and establish new strategic partners and clients.

The majority of our congress budget will be spent on the venue itself, logistics, travel and accommodation for our keynote speakers, poster awards, fellowships, catering and the social event. Therefore we appreciate all financial and in-kind support.

Please take a look at ECCB'12 sponsors or at the list of our previous sponsors.

Exhibitors and Sponsors Area

The exhibitors and sponsors area guarantee an excellent circulation of participants around the venue.The booths are next to the coffee break space and are directly in front of the main auditorium.
