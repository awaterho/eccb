ECCB'14 Banner Contest

  •  Print 

14 original banners are in competition for the Banner Contest.

Every ECCB'14 participant will receive a ballot to vote for his favorite banner. A ballot box will be available at the Registration and Information Desk.

  • A new banner is loaded randomly at each new connection to the eccb14.org site.
  • All 14 banners are visible here.

The winner will have the possibility to choose between an iPad and a free (refunded) registration.

 The vote will be closed Tuesday,  September 9th at 18:00 and the winner will be announced during the evening gala.

 

The ECCB'14 Banner Contest is sponsored by the LORIA: Lorraine Laboratory of Computer Science and its Applications

 

 

 

Details of the Call

Call for submissions to the Banner Contest is closed since May 5th, 2014

 

For its 2014 edition, the ECCB organizing committee invites you to compete for the best web banner dedicated to the ECCB 2014 web site.

1. Prepare an artistic jpeg banner in relation with ECCB topics and venue.

  • dimensions required : 1843px X 207px
  • maximum size : 800 kbytes
  • ECCB logo required (to be downloaded here)
  • only free-of-charge pictures (source required)
  • only one submission per participant will be accepted

2. Send the jpeg banner to This email address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it. before May 5th, 2014 (early registration opening) with your name, e-mail and address

3. If the banner meets all requirement it will be posted on the website (a new banner will appear at each connection)

4. A vote will be organized during early registration phase (from May 5th to August 1st, 2014) : one vote per registration

5. The prize for the winner is offered by the LORIA (Lorraine Laboratory of Research in Computer Science and its Applications) will be an iPAD or a free (refunded) registration to the conference ( :) ).

Take your chance ! Express yourself ! Bring web art into science and science into web art !

We thank you in advance for participating and embellishing our web site !

Marie-Dominique Devignes

On behalf of the organizing committee
