ECCB'14 awards have been announced during the closing ceremony of ECCB'14 on Wednesday September 10th. 

Best Proceedings Paper

PP21 - Chris Oates, Frank Dondelinger, Nora Bayani, James Korkola, Joe Gray and Sach Mukherjee. Causal network inference using biochemical kinetics.  [pdf]

Honorable Proceedings Papers

In addition to the best paper, the best paper committee selected two very honorable papers:

  • PP03 - Andrea Gobbi, Francesco Iorio, Kevin J. Dawson, David C. Wedge, David Tamborero, Ludmil B. Alexandrov, Nuria Lopez-Bigas, Mathew J. Garnett, Giuseppe Jurman and Julio Saez-Rodriguez. Fast randomisation of large genomic datasets while preserving alteration counts.  [pdf]
  • PP04 - Thorsten Will and Volkhard Helms. Identifying transcription factor complexes and their roles.  [pdf]

Best Poster Award

2 posters ex aequo received the best poster award:

  • D05 - Isaac Crespo et al.: Angiogenic activity of breast cancer patients’ monocytes reverted by combined use of systems modeling and experimental approaches
  • D22 - Samuel Collombet et al.: Logical modelling of immune cell specification and reprogramming
