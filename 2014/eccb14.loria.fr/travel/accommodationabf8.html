Accommodation in Strasbourg

  •  Print 

IMPORTANT : Participants to the ECCB’14  conference should make their own hotel reservations.

RECOMMENDATIONS

  • First, book your room as soon as possible!

    Strasbourg is a city where the hotels are almost always full (tourism, sessions of the European Parliament, congresses, etc.)

  • Second, prefer a hotel in the city center!

    It is more convenient for your evenings in Strasbourg :) .
