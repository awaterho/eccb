ECCB'2003 European Conference on Computational Biology
September 27-30, 2003, Paris, FRANCE

POSTERS SUBMISSION

poster abstract submission has been closed on June 9, 2003
all 286 poster abstracts have been accepted, and appear here
some of them (21) have been selected for flash presentations.

All posters abstracts have been accepted, they will be printed in the ECCB'2003 Proceedings which will be distributed to all participants. At least one of the authors must be present at the Conference during the posters sessions.

Instructions for authors:
All posters will be displayed throughout the conference, they should be placed on the panels on the first day of the conference. First poster session is on sunday in the morning (see detailed program).
The poster panels are vertical wood panels, self standing, recto/verso.
The size of the poster space is: 1.80m height * 0.94m width. At your arrival you will find all you need to fix your abstract poster on the boards.

Please consult the page www.inra.fr/eccb2003/posters.htm to know your poster number.

Thank's for your contribution and see you soon in Paris!
