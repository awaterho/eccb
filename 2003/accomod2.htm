Reservation conditions
You will receive all you need for a successful check in
  • An Invoice stating the amount already paid as a deposit.
  • A Voucher which you must show at check in.

    All the necessary information concerning your stay will be clearly stated.
    The voucher sent, should be considered as a guarantee for your reservation.

    Upon check out you will be requested to settle with the hotel the remaining amount stated on your voucher.
    Of course all extras are to be paid separately (meals, minibar, telephone).

    • Cancellation policy
    For all reservations we require a non refundable deposit.
    This deposit may vary depending on the hotel you wish to stay at.
    The deposit is equal to 20 to 30 % of the total stay depending the hotel chosen.
    You will receive the exact quote with your confirmation.

    If you have to shorten your stay, please note that all prepaid funds are not refundable.
    Also, if you have to lengthen your stay, you will be required a deposit for the additional days.

    Last thing, all cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before your initial arrival date.
    Failure to do so will induce a late cancellation penalty to the equivalent of one night's stay in addition to the deposit.
