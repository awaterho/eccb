Registration

Registration for ECCB 2002 has opened June 1, 2002

Deadline for early registration is August 1, 2002

The registration fee includes
  • free access to all talks and poster sessions of the conference as well as the exhibition and business meeting on Monday evening
  • a free access to the reception on Sunday October 6, 2002
  • a copy pf the proceedings and of the poster booklet
  • free lunches on October 7 - 9, 2002
The social event of the conference on Tuesday October 8, 2002 can be booked at an extra fee.
