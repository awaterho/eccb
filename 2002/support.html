Financial support for graduate students:



We are happy to announce that some financial support is available for travel to the ECCB 2002.

ISCB is awarding travel fellowships for students that are ISCB members. Please contact admin@iscb.org.

GBM offers financial support for young members of the society. Please contact Joerg Maxton-Kuechenmeister.

ECCB 2002 itself will be able to award a few travel fellowships to graduate students.
Preference will be given to students who will present their work at the conference, as papers or posters.
Deadline for applications for ECCB 2002 travel fellowships is August 15, 2002.

Please send by email a short (1 page) description including:
  • Name
  • Affiliation
  • Ph.D. Advisor name
  • Expected graduation date
  • Are you presenting a paper at ECCB 2002?
  • Have you submitted a poster to ECCB 2002?
  • A short description of your research area and your work

ECCB 2002 will announce its travel awards by September 1, 2002.

Only one of the three alternatives above can be chosen.
