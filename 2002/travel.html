How to reach the Congress Hall

The "Congresshalle" is located in the center of Saarbrücken at the Hafenstraße. Saarbrücken has its own airport (Saarbrücken-Ensheim) and is tightly connected by train or shuttle bus to the airports at Luxemburg and Frankfurt. Major railroads connect Saarbrücken every hour within Germany and frequently to Metz, Nancy or Paris. Highways lead to Mannheim/Frankfurt, Luxemburg/Trier/Köln, Strassbourg or Metz/Nancy/Paris.


The "Congresshalle" can be reached...
  • from Saarbrücken-Ensheim, Airport
    by taxi approx. 30 min. ca. €20

  • from Saarbrücken, Main Station
    by foot approx. 5 min

  • from Frankfurt or Mannheim
    via Autobahn A6 by car
    leaving at exit "Westspange"
    following the signs "Congresshalle"

  • from Paris via Autobahn A4 by car
    leaving at exit "Westspange"
    following the signs "Congresshalle"

  • from Trier via Autobahn A1, A8, A623 heading to Saarbrücken
