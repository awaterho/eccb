Fellowships

Fellowship application is closed


Several organizations support ECCB by granting fellowships.
ISCB fellowships can be granted to students / postdocs who are ISCB members presenting a paper or a poster at the Conference.
In the same way BioSapiens and EMBRACE Network of Excellence fellowships can be granted to students / postdocs presenting a paper or a poster at the Conference.
ECCB volunteer fellowships can be granted to students who accept to help at the Conference (see guidelines) also without presenting any paper or poster. Grantees will be communicated the source of support and will have to acknowledge it in the paper / poster.

Travel and volunteer fellowships will pay for airfare (limited amount), lodging, conference registration. Fellowships will not cover affiliated expenses, such as evening meals, workshops, tutorials, conference outing.

Airfare: The airfare portion of the award will depend on your geographical location; the maximum amount that you will be reimbursed will be included in your award letter, and might be less than the cost of your ticket.
We expect to be able to cover approximately 200 € for people coming from Italy, 600 € for the rest of Europe and 900 € in all other cases. You will need to pay for your airfare up front, and a reimbursement check will be mailed to you approximately three weeks after the end of the conference.

Lodging: The Conference Secretariat will arrange lodging for fellowship recipients, for the duration of the meeting, with two people per room. Fellowship recipients should not make their own lodging arrangements. You will not need to pay for lodging, but you will be responsible for affiliated expenses such as phone calls from your room.

Conference registration: Your registration fee will be reduced to 100 €.

Meals: Breakfasts will be included with your lodging and conference registration includes lunch, so most participants will only have to pay for suppers.

NOTE: We intend to support as many applicants as possible. Unfortunately, because of limited funds, we will not be able to provide support to all applicants. Applying for a fellowship does not guarantee that you will receive funding – if you require a fellowship in order to attend, please do not register until after you have been notified that you have received an award.


KEY DATES

June 3, 2008: Travel and Volunteer Fellowship Application Process Opens
June 20, 2008: Travel and Volunteer Fellowship Application Deadline
July 3, 2008: Travel and Volunteer Fellowship Acceptance Notification
August 22, 2008: Conference early-registration deadline

Travel Fellowships

The 7th European Conference on Computational Biology (ECCB08) will provide travel fellowships to help students and postdocs to attend the conference which will be held on September 22 – 26, 2008, in Cagliari, Italy, in the island of Sardinia


REQUIREMENTS

  1. Applicant must be the presenting author of an accepted Paper or Poster* at ECCB08. Application may be submitted before acceptance notification is received.
    *Limit of one poster per person and multiple authors applying with the same poster title will not be considered.
  2. Applicant must be registered in a degree program (undergraduate or graduate) or as a postdoctoral research fellow at an accredited university or college at the time of the conference. Evidence of eligibility (e.g., a student ID card) must be presented when submitting receipts.
  3. The applicant must register for ECCB08 shortly after receiving notification of the fellowship award. The deadline for registering will be included in the award letter.
  4. Funded applicant must submit receipts for travel that equal or exceed the announced award amount in order to receive the full award funding. Details of receipt submission and check distribution will be included in acceptance letter.

Instructions
  1. Complete and submit the Travel Fellowship application form (available starting June 3, 2008).

  2. If you need to make changes to your application, please send an e-mail to info@eccb08.org; do not submit another application.

  3. You will be notified before July 3, 2008 if your application has been approved for funding. Funded applicants will be required to present evidence of their eligibility status (such as student identification card).

  4. All travel fellowship recipients will be required to register and pay in advance for ECCB08 (Registration will begin April 28, 2008, and the early registration discount will end August 22, 2008). If your attendance at the ECCB08 conference is dependent on receipt of a travel fellowship, please do not register until after the notification of travel fellowships.

  5. IMPORTANT REMINDER: You must submit a Paper or a Poster before you submit a travel fellowship application!

Deadline: fellowship applicants must complete and submit the following form no later than June 20, 2008, by 12:00 AM GMT.

Questions regarding fellowships should be addressed to: info@eccb08.org

Volunteer Opportunities

We are looking for student volunteers to work at the 7th European Conference on Computational Biology (ECCB08), from September 22nd to September 26th in Cagliari, Italy, in the island of Sardinia.
This will give many of you a chance to network with some of the world's most outstanding computational biologists and to learn a whole lot more about the fast-growing field of bioinformatics.
The ECCB08 Organizing Committee would like to invite students to take a more active role in making ECCB08 a successful conference by volunteering their services.


REQUIREMENTS


  1. Applicant must be registered in a degree program (undergraduate or graduate) at an accredited university or college at the time of the conference. Evidence of eligibility (e.g., a student ID card) must be presented when submitting receipts.
  2. The applicant must register for ECCB08 shortly after receiving notification of the fellowship award. The deadline for registering will be included in the award letter.
  3. Funded applicant must submit receipts for travel that equal or exceed the announced award amount in order to receive the full award funding. Details of receipt submission and check distribution will be included in acceptance letter.


Jobs include general assistance. In particular we need people to help out for things like passing microphones around to audience questioners, helping speakers transfer their presentation media between computers, poster assistance, etc.
Volunteers are expected to assist for approximately 2 sessions of 4-5 hours during the conference dates. Volunteers are expected to be in the conference hall on Monday 22nd September for istructions.

In return, volunteers will receive the following “freebies”:

  • Travel contribution (see above)
  • Reduced registration fee: you will pay only € 100
  • Free T-shirt(s) (which must be worn throughout the conference) & Conference Materials. 
  • Free lunches/snacks/coffee/tea
  • Opportunity to Hear/Meet/Visit with some World Famous Scientists!!
  • Accommodation in a bed & breakfast, shared room (double room).
 
Instructions
  1. Complete and submit the Travel Fellowship application form (available starting June 3, 2008).

  2. If you need to make changes to your application please send an e-mail to info@eccb08.org; Do not submit another application.

  3. You will be notified before July 3, 2008 if your application has been approved for funding. Funded applicants will be required to present evidence of their eligibility status (such as student identification card).

  4. All the accepted volunteer recipients will be required to register and pay in advance for ECCB 2008 (Registration will begin April 27, 2008, and the early registration discount will end August 22, 2008). If your attendance at the ECCB 2008 conference is dependent on receipt of a fellowship, please do not register until after the notification of fellowships.

IMPORTANT: if you apply as a volunteer you will be contacted by a member of the local organizing committee by phone before a decision is made. So please be sure to correctly supply your phone number and your e-mail address.
