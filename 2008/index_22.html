Accommodation

ECCB08 pre-booked hotel rooms can only be reserved using the registration form.
The organising secretariat has arranged for accomodation at various hotel in Cagliari city (hotels are located in the town centre and within 5/6 km from the Conference Centre); booking service is reserved to regularly registered participants but  reservation should be done before June 30, 2008. The availability of rooms might be limited and cannot be guaranteed after June 30, 2008; therefore we advise to book well in advance.

A shuttle bus will be available to provide transportation between the conference center and local hotels.

Rates are per room, per night, including breakfast, taxes and service. 
B&B rooms should be booked by attendees individually.  You can find below ECCB08  hotel rates and a link to several Cagliari B&B sites.

Some hotels have special rates for the period before and after the conference (with some conditions).  Participants who wish to extend their stay should contact the hotel directly to reserve the extra days.

For further details contact:
