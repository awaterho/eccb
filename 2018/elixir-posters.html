CALL FOR ELIXIR POSTERS â€“ ECCB 2018

The ELIXIR poster session will present the full scope of ELIXIR technical and scientific activities. We invite researchers, developers and infrastructure service providers from ELIXIR Nodes to present their services and activities. The call is open to authors from institutes that are official partners of ELIXIR or the ELIXIR-EXCELERATE project.

The deadline for submission is 24 June 2018 (11:59 p.m. in the time zone of your choice), authors will be notified by 17 July 2018.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

All submissions should be:

  1. Relevant to ELIXIR technical and scientific activities
  2. Relevant to the conference programme

All submissions that meet the above criteria will be accepted to the ELIXIR Poster session. The ultimate decision of acceptance for a poster lies with the ELIXIR Advisory Board of the ELIXIR F1000Research Gateway.

The presenting author of a poster must be a registered delegate and be available at the poster session to which their poster is assigned. Please register before the online registration deadline on August 19, 2018. The early bird registration deadline is set on July 29, 2018.

Posters submitted by the deadline will be notified of acceptance by 17 July 2018. Poster abstracts (maximum 250 words) will be posted on the conference web site. A poster that is judged by the conference organizers to be an advertisement will be subject to removal without notice.

PRESENTATION GUIDELINES

The poster size should be 84.1 x 118.9 cm (A0 format). Authors are encouraged to use the ELIXIR poster templates:

  • EXCELERATE poster template (for ELIXIR-EXCELERATE activities)
  • ELIXIR poster template (for all other ELIXIR activities)
