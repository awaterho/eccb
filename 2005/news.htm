2005/04/01

Paper Reviewing Stage For ECCB/JBI'05 Has Begun.

There have been 209 papers submitted to the ECCB/JBI'05 meeting.
Among those, there are 157 long papers and 52 short papers.
The paper revision stage has just begun, it will last from April 1st to April 30th. We expect to notify acceptance or rejection of the papers to their authors about May 16th.

KEY DATES
April 1/30 Reviewing phase.
May 16 Notification to authors of accepted/rejected papers.
May 30 Deadline for submission of accepted papers final version.
