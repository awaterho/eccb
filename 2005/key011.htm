FEDERICO MORÁN

 moranaf@solea.quim.ucm.es
   
Postdoctoral research at the department of "Physique-Chemie II" at the Universite Livre de Bruxelles, Belgium, on complex dynamics of biochemical reactions. Continue research activities at UCM, University California Irvine, University of Southern California, and Standford University. The subject of research includes the study of complex biochemical networks, molecular evolution, and origin of selfmaintaned cell in the context of prebiotic evolution. He is familiar with both genetic algorithms and unsupervised neural networks techniques.

Current Position: Profesor Titular of Biophysics, Department of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology, UCM. Lecturer of Biophysics at the UCM. Senior Scientist and foundational member of the Centro de Astrobiología, CAB(CSIC-INTA), associated to NASA Astrobiology Institute. Director of the Molecular Evolution Laboratory at CAB. Associate Director of the Spanish Institute for Bioinformatics.
