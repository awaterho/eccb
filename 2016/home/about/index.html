About

about ECCB 2016

On 3-7 September 2016 the Dutch Techcentre for Life Sciences, the BioSB research school and ELIXIR will jointly organise the fifteenth edition of the European Conference on Computational Biology in association with the Vrij Universiteit Amsterdam and the Delft University of Technology. The ECCB 2016 will take place in the historic city of The Hague in the Netherlands.
It is the main computational event in 2016 and will welcome scientists working in a variety of disciplines, including bioinformatics, computational biology, biology, medicine, and systems biology. Participating in ECCB 2016 will be the perfect opportunity to keep pace with cutting edge research, and to network with members of ECCB community.

sponsorship opportunities

ECCB 2016 is the perfect opportunity to strengthen your visibility and network with key opinion leaders and decision makers in the field of computational life sciences and big data analytics. It gives your organisation an ideal platform for demonstrating innovative technologies, life science applications, software tools, services and training initiatives. Looking for the next generation of data experts? ECCB 2016 gathers the crème de la crème of life science data experts and talented students that can help you tackle the growing data and ICT challenges in your R&D. ECCB 2016 offers a number of advertising and sponsoring options, for you to present yourself to an international audience of over 1000 scientists, policy makers, and other stakeholders.
