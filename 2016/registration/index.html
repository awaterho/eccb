group discount

Register 6 delegates and receive one free ECCB 2016 conference registration!

Individuals from the same lab or organisation have an opportunity to receive a complimentary 7th conference registration when registering as a group. The free registration is for the main conference and does not include additional registration options (i.e. workshops, tutorials, accompanying persons, etc.)

To benefit from this special offer, the group should simply identify a group leader and have her/him contact the ECCB 2016 registration team at eccb@congressbydesign.com. The registration team will coordinate the group’s registration following the normal registration process.

The complimentary registration is applicable to the lowest registration fee and the discount is only applicable before the early registration deadline (29 July 2016). Please note that the delegate will be charged for additional conference activities not covered by this special offer, or in the case of a cancellation of a group member.

Cancellation Policy

In case of cancellation before or on 3 August, the registration fee minus handling costs will be refunded. If cancellation is received after 3 August, or if the applicant fails to attend the conference for any reason whatsoever, no refund can be made. Cancellation of registration has to be made in writing to the Conference secretariat ECCB 2016 (by email: eccb@congressbydesign.com).

Handling costs

  • cancellation main conference (incl. pre-meetings if applicable): €75,-
  • cancellation pre-meeting(s) only: €35,-

Payment policy

Credit card

The registration system will take you to the secured site of our credit card payment service, Ogone. Your payment will then be made to the ECCB 2016 account of Congress by Design, the conference service agency of this congress. We accept MasterCard, Visa and American Express cards. All payments are in EUR.

After your payment is approved your registration is finalized. You will be given the option to download or print a proof of payment. Any additional fees (e.g. bank charges) are at own expense.

Invoice

Payment of the applicable registration fee must have been made within 14 days after registration to secure your participation. Any additional fees (e.g. bank charges) are at own expense. All payments are in EUR and we do not accept payments by check.
