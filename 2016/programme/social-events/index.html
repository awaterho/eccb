Social events

Welcome reception

Place: Gemeentemuseum The Hague
Date: Sunday September 4, 19:30-21:00
Price delegates: Included in the registration fee
Price accompanying persons: EUR 100

Gemeentemuseum Den Haag features the world’s largest collection of works by Piet Mondrian. In the spectacular Mondrian & De Stijl wing, you can witness Piet Mondrian’s development from a landscape painter to a pioneer of modern art. You can also see how the designers of De Stijl were the forerunners of present-day design. The museum also displays works by top 20th-century artists such as Van Gogh, Monet and Picasso, and has one of the world’s finest and largest collections of Delft Blue china.

The Gemeentemuseum Den Haag is located at a 2 minute walk from the Worldforum (conference venue), so come and join us for a drink and a bite after the opening lecture.

Yoga sessions

Place: World Forum
Date: Please find schedule below
Price delegates: Included in the registration fee
Price accompanying persons: free
Dress information: T-shirts available in case you need more comfortable clothing

Yoga to energize your body and mind
Thanks to iKeys we are pleased to offer our conference attendees free yoga sessions during the lunch breaks and poster sessions of ECCB 2016. Yoga does more than burn calories and tone muscles. It is a total mind-body workout that combines strengthening and stretching poses with deep breathing and meditation or relaxation. Never did a yoga workout before? Do not worry, the sessions will be adapted to the level of the attendees. People of all ages and fitness levels can do the most basic yoga poses and stretches. Yoga mats will be provided and there will be T-shirts available in case you need more comfortable clothing.

International Kanker en Yoga science (iKeys) is a foundation which provide funds to conduct Yoga therapy and research activity to improve the quality of life for all cancer survivors and patients. iKeys supports the world’s brightest and best researchers who relentlessly pursue the solutions that help us understand how to prevent the occurrence of cancers. They conduct and promote various activities to promote the
awareness of Yoga.

Orienteering Ice Breaking Event – The Hague @ Night Photo Tour

Place: The Hague city centre
Date: Monday 5 September, 19:30 – ca. 22:00
Price delegates: Included in the registration fee
Price accompanying persons: Free
Event meet-up: 19:30 – ca. 22:00
Event begins: 20:00 (approximately 120 minutes in length)
Dress code: Walking shoes and appropriate outdoor clothes

The annual orienteering ice breaking event – “The Hague @ Night Photo Tour” provides the delegates with an opportunity to explore this beautiful historic city and meet new people in a fun way. Teams are formed, and provided with a map, tourist information and a set of partial photos of The Hague’s most famous and interesting landmarks to discover. During the tour each team has the opportunity to have some food together. At the end of the tour all groups get together in a central bar in The Hague to compare results over drinks. This event has been made possible by the ISCB Student Council.

The Hague @ Night Photo Tour is free of cost, however transportation, food and drinks are on own cost.

 

Conference dinner

Place: Beachclub Doen!
Date: Tuesday September 6, 20:00-23:30
Price delegates: EUR 45
Price accompanying persons: EUR 90
Dress code: Casual clothing and shoes strongly recommended
Please note: There is a limited number of seats. Tickets cannot be purchased onsite.

Address
Beachclub Doen!
Strandweg 9
2586 JK Scheveningen

Lovely weather in September, so let’s go to the beach! This time ECCB’s conference dinner will take place on the beach, at the Beachclub Doen! in Scheveningen (The Hague).

The beachclub can be reached from the conference venue within 10 minutes travel time by tram or taxi. If you chose to walk you may need approximately 30 minutes. Of course you will receive detailed instructions closer to date. Please contact the organizers if you need further assistance.

The dinner at the beach starts at about 20:00 and will end at about 23:30 hours.

Beachclub Doen! is virtually located on the sand of the beach. It is an informal setting, with wooden paths and floors with, here and there, the beach sand. The Conference Dinner is of barbeque type. Therefore we strongly recommend casual clothing and shoes. Though we have ordered dry weather, if this does not work out you may have to walk a short distance in rain, from the tram or taxi stop to the beach club and/or back.

Please note that the restaurants along the Scheveningen Boulevard, such as the Beachclub Doen!, cannot be found by a street number. Beachclub Doen! is located at the height of the lighthouse, the needle and the endstop of tram 11.

Want to bring someone with you?
You would like to bring your partner or a friend with you? Please do, the more the merrier! All you have to do is to buy a ticket during online registration. Cost for attending the conference dinner only are € 90 and include VAT, food and drinks.

Invitation

We cordially invite you to visit European Conference on Computational Biology 2016 which will be hosted by the Dutch Royal city of peace and justice. The Hague is ready to welcome you and offer you the perfect balance of science and pleasure.
