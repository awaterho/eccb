Badges

All exhibitors must register with the Conference Registration Desk to receive an official ISMB/ECCB 2009 conference name badge. The badge will be required for admission to the Exhibition Hall. A special "private" registration site will be set up for exhibiting companies to register their personnel.

Conference Venue and Location

The conference and the exhibition will be held at the Stockholmsmässan – Stockholm International Fairs (SIF). The SIF can be reached by train from the conference hotels situated in the city’s center. The Rica Talk hotel is located adjacent to the SIF. The train ride takes approximately 10-15 minutes between the two locations.

Demonstrations

Demonstrations and other special activities must be located so that crowds will be comfortably contained within the contracted display space and not blocking any of the aisles. Distracting activities are subject to adjacent exhibitor and ISMB/ECCB approval. Exhibitors may not play loud music on their stand.

Exhibit Booth Package

ISMB/ECCB 2009 Exhibitors receive the following package:

• Display space, hard wall structure (3m x 3m)
• One (1) table (Measurement: 1500x800 mm. Height: 700 mm) and two (2) padded chairs
• One power outlet (220V 10 Amp) included in each booth
• Three (3) exhibit hall passes (no access to scientific sessions)
• Organization listed on conference website and in conference program book
• Company name on a sign, printed one color
• Wireless internet available in exhibit area
** Does not include conference registration
