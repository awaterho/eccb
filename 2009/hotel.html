THE FOLLOWING HOTELS HAVE BEEN PRE-BOOKED FOR THE ISMB/ECCB DELEGATES

 

 

 

Radisson SAS Royal Viking Hotel**** (head quarter hotel)

 

 

Radisson SAS Royal Viking is a modern four-star hotel. The hotel is located next to the central railway station and the Arlanda express. They have a Bermuda Pool Club with pool, Jacuzzi, sauna, bar and gym. The hotel includes Stockholm Fisk - a fish and seafood restaurant - and a SkyBar on the top floor with a view over the city. The hotel offers 24 hours Room Service.

 

Approximate travel time to Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs) Fairs: 15 minutes

 

Rates

Standard room single use SEK 2 020

Standard room double use SEK 2 020

 

 

Sheraton Stockholm Hotel*****

 

 

The Sheraton Stockholm Hotel & Towers is a five-star hotel located in the very heart of Stockholm's central business district, shopping areas and attractions - perfect for both business and leisure guests. The hotel offers stunning views and Lake Mälaren, City Hall and the Old Town. Hotel facilities include two restaurants, two bars, a mini gym and a sauna. The hotel offers 24-hour Room Service.

 

Approximate travel time to Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs): 15 minutes

 

Rates

Classic Single room SEK 2 170

Classic Double room SEK 2 370

Superior Singe room SEK 2 420

Superior Double room SEK 2 620

 

 

 

Nordic Sea Hotel****

 

Nordic Sea is a four-star modern hotel with maritime style. The hotel is located next to the central railway station and the Arlanda express. In this hotel you will also find the famous Ice-Bar which is the world's first permanent ice bar. The interior is kept at below zero temperature, -5°, all year round. All of the Ice-Bar's interior fittings, including the glasses, are made of pure, clear ice from the Torne River in northern Sweden. They also have the C-bar which offers light meals. The relaxation room of Nordic Hotels offers luxurious bathing machines, saunas and a mini gym. The hotel offers Room Service.

 

Approximate travel time to Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs): 15 minutes

 

Rates

Standard Single room SEK 2 595

Superior Single room SEK 2 810

 

 

Rica Hotel Kungsgatan****

 

This modern first class hotel is located in the very city centre of Stockholm, right in the middle of the shopping district. The hotel is situated on the 5 top floors of the well-known PUB department store building at Stockholm's oldest market place, Hötorget. Beautiful Scandinavian furnished rooms of high standard characterise the hotel. The hotel itself has no restaurant but collaborates with several restaurants in the neighbourhood, all of which have a wide range of international dishes. A snack menu is available in the lobby bar.

 

Approximate travel time to Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs): 15 minutes

 

Rates

Single room SEK 1 610

Double room SEK 1 860

 

 

Rica Hotel Stockholm****

 

 

Centrally located in the heart of Stockholm, you will find this newly renovated first class hotel. Situated by Hötorget, the flower market, the location is unbeatable; it is close to public transportation, shopping, theatres and other cultural events. Rica Hotel Stockholm also has conference facilities and the kitchen offers delicious home cooking for lunch.

 

Approximate travel time to Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs): 15 minutes

Rica Talk Hotel****

 

 

This multi award-winning, state-of-the-art, 248-room hotel is located just south of central Stockholm. Hotel rooms and suites, restaurant, bar, 24-hour-reception, car parking, conference facilities, gym and sauna are all under the same roof as the Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs) centre.

Hotel Tegnérlunden***

Hotel Tegnérlunden is fully booked.

 

 

The Hotel Tegnérlunden is positioned in downtown Stockholm next to Tegnérlunden Park. This six floor structure is two kilometres from the Royal Opera House, Royal Palace, National Museum of Fine Art and Old Town. The 102 guest rooms within the hotel are fashioned with pine flooring, soft peach-coloured walls, powder blue fabrics and wood furnishings decorate each room. Rooms include high-speed Internet access and satellite television. Additional amenities include desks and sitting areas.

 

Approximate travel time to Stockholmsmässan (Stockholm International Fairs): 20 minutes
