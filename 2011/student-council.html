What is the Student Council?

ISCB Student Council was officially approved by ISCB Board of Directors during ISMB 2004. The ISCB Student Council is a section of ISCB dedicated to facilitating opportunities of development for students and young researchers in the field of Computational Biology and Bioinformatics.
