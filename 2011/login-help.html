Login Instructions

The login fields are in the upper right corner of the site. You must be an ISCB member to login. If you are not a member please JOIN NOW.

By default the username is set to the email address you used during the registration process. Optionally you can personalize your username after you login by following these steps:

-> Click on the "Membership" tab

-> Click "Update My Profile" in the left handed menu "ISCB User Menu"

-> Select "User Info"

-> Edit the Username by changing it to a name of your choice
(username must be at least 3 characters and no spaces).

-> Click "Update" button


From now on the chosen username must be used for future login processes.
