BIRDS OF A FEATHER Signup

ISMB/ECCB 2011 will offer a number of "spontaneous" open meetings for participants to meet and talk about topics of mutual interest. The "Birds-of-a-feather" (BoF) meetings are a long-standing, highly successful tradition at ISMB. During the conference, participants are encouraged to submit a topic for discussion to this website and to recruit as many (or as few) interested participants as desired by the topic leader. The conference organizers have set aside several rooms specifically for birds-of-a-feather meetings.

Birds of a Feather announcements and sign up sheets (Topic, Topic Leader, Location, Time) will be announced each day.
