MEDIA INVITATION - Complimentary Press Pass

ISMB/ECCB 2011 invites accredited media working for scientific news organizations to apply for a complimentary press pass. This premier event for computational biology will provide members of the media access to three information packed days with a wide range of scientific presentations. In addition, you will have access to hundreds of scientific posters and numerous exhibitors.

Press passes are limited, and are granted only to working journalists who intend to cover the event. To qualify for press credentials, you must be affiliated with an internationally, nationally or regionally recognized media outlet, and hold an editorial title and or hold a position as an industry analyst. Personnel that are in sales, advertising, marketing, account management, or other non-editorial professions will not be granted a press pass. (ISCB reserves the right to request documentation regarding credentials.)

ISMB/ECCB 2011 is presented as the 19th annual meeting of the International Society for Computational Biology (ISCB). Conference details on past Intelligent Systems for Molecular Biology are available through this link- http://www.iscb.org/iscb-past-conferences

ISMB/ECCB 2011 will offer the strongest scientific program and the broadest scope of any international bioinformatics conference in 2011. Building on the success of past conferences, the Vienna conference is designed to cater to a variety of disciplines within the bioinformatics community, resulting in the largest and most important computational biology event globally.

Please reference the above in published articles. ISCB will require copies of all published articles.
