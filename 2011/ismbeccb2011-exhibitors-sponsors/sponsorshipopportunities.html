Sponsorship Opportunities

** Sponsor Sign-up**
 

Sign up Early and be Entered in a Drawing to Win!! Congratulations to:

The European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI), the winner of 4 nights accommodation at the Hilton!!

 

ISMB/ECCB attracts top international scientists and key decision makers in the life sciences - experts in areas such as computer science, molecular biology and medicine, mathematics and statistics - from the world's largest and most prestigious research institutions. These leaders have come to see the conferences as an opportunity to seek out and learn about the new tools, technologies, publications and services featured by sponsors and exhibitors. ISMB/ECCB 2011 returns to Vienna at the Austria Center and looks forward to welcoming your organization and the opportunity to reach this global market in one place at one time.

Sponsorship options have been designed for every budget level and the organizers can arrange a flexible solution for your marketing requirements. By committing now you can take full advantage of this opportunity to enhance your organization's visibility, expand your network of prospects, meet up with your current clients and positively impact your bottom line sales at ISMB/ECCB .

We look forward to your participation in Vienna!
