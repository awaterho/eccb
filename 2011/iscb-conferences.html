LATIN AMERICA 2012
The second ISCB Latin America meeting takes place March 17-21, 2012, in Santiago, Chile. The major aims of this regional meeting are to deeply motivate and inspire Latin American students and post-docs to conduct the best research possible by bringing together an exceptional list of international and regional keynote presenters, and to attract all levels of researchers from around the world to share projects and form collaborations…Read more. . .

GLBIO 2012
The 2nd ISCB Great Lakes Bioinformatics Conference is hosted by University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, May 15 - 17, 2012.  The conference provides an interdisciplinary forum for the discussion of research findings and methods. An important goal is to foster long term collaborative relationships and networking opportunities within the domain of computational approaches to biology. Read more. . .

ISMB 2012
ISMB celebrates its 20th anniversary in 2012! A spectacular meeting is planned for beautiful Long Beach, California, USA, July 15 - July 17, with pre-conference events starting on the 13th and post-conference events continuing through the 19th. The conference features multiple tracks of scientific presentations, hundreds of posters, and internationally renowned keynote presenters including ISCB's annual Overton Prize and Sr. Scientist Award winners.  Read more. . .
