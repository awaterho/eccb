Poster Sessions

 

Posters should be put up on the morning of Monday 10 September and remain posted until the end of the meeting on Wednesday 12 September. Presenters of odd numbered posters should be present at their poster during the first poster session on Monday afternoon, presenters of even numbered posters during the poster session on Tuesday afternoon. You can find your poster number in fornt of your poster abstract.

 

Each poster will have a space of 1.20 meter wide by 1.50 meters tall. The conference will provide material for hanging posters.

 

Poster Printing Service

 

In case you don't want to carry your poster during your air travel to Basel, "Creastampa" provides a local poster printing service (special price CHF 65 until 5th September; express surcharge CHF 25 until 7th September). Posters will be delivered directly to the Congress Center. More information at http://www.creastampa.ch.

 

Extend the Life of your Poster


ECCB 2012, in cooperation with Faculty of 1000, invites all poster presenters to deposit their poster presentation(s) into the established open access poster repository, F1000 Posters, to allow those who could not make the meeting have the opportunity to see your novel work. F1000's expert Faculty of 10,000 members will then view these submissions to identify those they wish to select for positive evaluation in the award winning F1000 service. To deposit your work, go to http://f1000.com/posters/depositor and upload your file to extend the life of your ECCB'12 presentation.

 

For more information for depositors, e.g. on copyright or prior publication questions, please visit the F1000 Posters website "For Depositors".
