Activities in Switzerland

 

Some ideas what to do before and after the ECCB meeting in Switzerland:

Swiss Alps Jungfrau-Aletsch.

Experience unique panoramas on the bus journey from Grindelwald along the UNESCO World Heritage site of the Jungfrau-Aletsch region.

www.sbb.ch

Swiss Museum of Transport Lucerne.

Travelling with kids, or still young at heart? The Swiss Museum of Transport presents the history of transport and mobility with a varied range of interactive exhibits. 

www.verkehrshaus.ch

La Gruyere: Mountains, cheese, and science fiction.

At the foot of the Pre-Alps, you will be enchanted by the charm and picturesque architecture of the medieval town of Gruyères. The town, which has given its name to the area and to its delicious cheese, also hosts the HR Giger museum - a Swiss artist, who won the Oscar for Best Visual Effects for his participation in the film "Alien".

www.la-gruyere.ch

Ticino
The Italian flair of Switzerland. The Mediterranean region seems to begin on the southern side of the Alps. There is a feel of Italy, with palm trees at clean beaches and lanes and alleyways leading to piazzas and churches.

www.ticino.ch
