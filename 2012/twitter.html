Tweetiquette @ECCB12

 

Here are some simple guidelines for tweeting and blogging at ECCB'12:

 

Hashtag

 

The official hashtag for tweeting from the European Conference on Computational Biology 2012 is #eccb12

 

 

Oral presentations

 

Tweeting and blogging from oral presentations is encouraged - unless the speaker explicitly disagrees.

 

@Speakers: If you do not want to be cited by your audience in tweets and blogs, please clearly state this explictly at the beginning of your talk.

 

@Twitters: Please do respect the wish of the speaker.

 

 

poster presentations

 

ECCB'12 considers poster presentations as "personal communication". There is an open source repository where poster authors can deposit their poster if they wish to make it public.

 

@Twitters: Please do not tweet photos of posters without the explicit consent of the author.
