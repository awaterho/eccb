Group discount

 

Register 6 and receive one complimentary student registration

 

Register 6 delegates and receive one free ECCB'12 student conference registration! Individuals from the same lab or group have an opportunity to receive a complimentary 7th student conference registration. To benefit from this special offer, the group leader should contact the ECCB'12 registration team at registration@eccb12.org, providing the names of the 6 already registered participants, and the data of the student receiving the complimentary registration using the following form [PDF].

 

Free registration is for the main conference and does not include additional registration options (i.e. workshops, tutorials, accompanying persons, etc..) Please note that the delegate will be charged for additional  conference activities not covered by this special offer, or in the case of a cancellation of a group member. The group discount is applicable only before the early registration deadline (1 August 2012).

 

 

Payment

 

Credit Card:

The default form to pay ECCB'12 registration fee is by credit card (Visa or Eurocard/MasterCard) via the online registration system. All payments are in Swiss Francs (CHF).

 

Wire transfer:

If for any reason credit card payment is not possible for you, please contact the registration secretariat (registration@eccb12.org) to arrange for payment by wire transfer. The wire transfer option is provided only for customers who are not able to pay by other means. Payment must be received in full and in Swiss Francs (CHF).

 

Your bank may charge fees for this service. Please note that all bank charges must be paid by the participant. Please indicate clearly the name of the participant, the conference ‘ECCB 2012’, and the registration code you received from the secretariat in all your remittances.

 

PLEASE NOTE: Wire transfers can take up to ten business days to process. Please allow for ample time for your transfer to reach us. You are not registered for the meeting until ECCB'12 receives your payment. Early registration fees must be received by the appropriate early registration deadline.

 

 

Letter of invitation

 

If you need a confirmation of registration for visa or funding purposes, please contact the ECCB'12 registration secretariat registration@eccb12.org by email. Please note that confirmations of registration are only issues after the registration in complete (i.e. all registration fees have been received).

 

 

Terms and conditions

 

Cancellations of confirmed registrations for the main conference, workshops, tutorials or satellite meetings  are subject to an adminstrative charge of 150 CHF per participant provided the cancellation is received by August 15, 2012. There will be no refunds for cancellations received after August 15, 2012 or for no-shows. Cancellations need to be made in writing to the registration secretariat.
