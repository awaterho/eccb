Registration
The early registration discount applies to registrations received on or before November 21, 2006 (Honolulu time – the latest timezone). The registration fee covers participation in all scientific sessions, conference materials, a welcome reception, and lunches on January 22-24. The registration fee for Israeli participants includes VAT.

ISCB membership
Members of the International Society for Computational Biology (ISCB) are entitled to discounted registration fees. Visit the ISCB website to join the Society; please do this before registering for ECCB.

Festive dinner
The festive dinner, on Tuesday evening, January 23, will be an enjoyable experience that reflects the local landscape and culture.

Proceedings
This year, the proceedings will be distributed to all delegates on CD. Those who desire a printed copy as well can order one. Orders for printed copies are due by the early registration deadline because of publishing deadlines. The deadline for ordering hardcopy proceedings has passed.


Workshops and tutorials
Two full-day workshops and three half-day tutorials will be offered. The fee includes participation in the relevant workshop or tutorials, workshop or tutorial materials, and lunch on January 21. Registration is for specific workshops or tutorials; registering for one does not provide access to others. Click here for details about workshops and tutorials.

Extra welcome receptions and lunches
People accompanying conference delegates can purchase tickets for the welcome reception and for lunches (Sunday through Wednesday). Please purchase tickets (price for lunch: $ 22) using the registration form.

Registration cancellation policy
If written cancellation is received by fax or e-mail at least 21 days prior to the conference, a full refund will be made, less a handling fee of US$30. Thereafter, no refunds will be available.

Domestic flights to / from Eilat
Eilat is conveniently accessible by air. Domestic flights to Eilat depart from Ben Gurion International Airport (BGU) and from Sde Dov (SDV) Airport in Tel Aviv. Travel time from BGU to SDV by taxi is approximately 30 minutes, and costs approx. US$30. Eilat airport is about a 5-10 minute drive from the conference hotels. The Dan Eilat hotel provides a free shuttle service; a taxi from Eilat airport to the conference hotels costs approx. US$5.

Please note that international flights arrive at Ben-Gurion Internation Airport - Terminal 3.
Domestic flights to Eilat depart from terminal 2, and there is a shuttle service connecting both terminals. When booking your flights, please allow 2.5 to 3 hours between arrival at BGU and departure to Eilat from BGU. You should take into consideration possible arrival delays, passport control, baggage claim, transfer to Terminal 2, and check in 45 minutes (from SDV) or 60 minutes (from BGU) prior to departure to Eilat.

Flight duration: approx. 50 minutes.

If your international flight to Israel arrives late in the evening or your return flight leaves early in the morning, you will probably want to use a hotel in Tel Aviv for the relevant night. The earliest flights between Tel Aviv and Eilat are at 6:30 a.m.; the last flights are at 21:00.

Optional Day Tours
Tour A:
Massada and Dead Sea
Sunday, January 21, 2007
Full Day Tour: 08:00-17:30

Drive through the Negev via the Arava highway to the ancient Herodian fortress of Massada, where you will hear the tragic story of the Zealots, its last defenders. Enjoy a special “swimming” adventure in the saltiest body of water in the world, the Dead Sea, which is also lowest point on earth!
Price per person: US$ 110 (Lunch included)
The tour will operate with a minimum of 15 participants.
The tour will depart from the Dan Hotel Lobby.


Tour B:
Coral World Underwater Observatory
Monday, January 22, 2007
Half Day Tour: 09:00-13:00

Visit the amazing underwater universe of the Red Sea without getting wet! From within the Coral World Underwater Observatory you can observe the wondrous world of the Red Sea: a living museum, 20 feet under the waves. The tour includes entrance to the Park, the Oceanarium, and the Glass Boat.
Price per person: US$ 40
The tour will operate with a minimum of 15 participants.
The tour will depart from the Dan Hotel Lobby.


Tour C:
The Ultimate Mountains and Desert Jeep Tour
Monday, January 22, 2007
Half Day: 14:30-19:00

This tour explores the changing desert landscape and will visit: Solomon's Valley, Eilat Pilgrim Pass, Mt. Yoash, the Spring of  Ein Netafim and more.  An exciting off-road adventure takes you over the most scenic 4x4 route in the Eilat Mountain Nature Reserve. The tour (in open jeeps) follows in part, ancient pilgrim and merchant routes. It ascends the highest summit in the area, where the breathtaking views take in 4 neighboring countries. The tour visits the only water source for wildlife in the vicinity and as the sun sets you can often see Ibexes herds coming to drink. We stop en route to enjoy desert style hospitality. Sitting around a campfire, on which Bedouin pita bread is made, we drink sweet tea and hear traditional desert tales, both true and legendary.
Price per person: US$ 45
The tour will operate with a minimum of 5 participants.
The tour will depart from the Dan Hotel Lobby.

Tour E:
Eilat Mountains Desert Tour - Camels and Jeeps Combined
Wednesday, January 24, 2007
Full Day: 08:30-17:30

Setting off from Eilat at 8:30 in jeeps, we will journey off road into the sandy Arava Valley, passing salt pools that are home to flamingos. From there we head west and begin our ascent to the Negev Plateau on a dramatic 4x4 route. We will drive on the way through the beautiful Wadi Eteq, where we will stop to explore by foot the Eteq Canyon, a favorite spot for local wildlife and home to many varieties of desert plants.

At the top of the Negev Plateau we will enjoy a picnic lunch before getting back into the jeeps and continuing on to Shacharut. This small village is perched on the edge of the huge cliffs where the Negev Plateau plummets into the Arava Valley. Here we will enjoy tea and coffee before mounting our camels for a walk/ride along one of the most scenic camel routes in the Negev Desert. We will walk and ride at a leisurely pace for about two and a half hours, taking in the breathtaking panoramic views and witnessing the most magical hour of desert life as the sunset sends a blaze of color across the desert sky. We will return at dusk by way of a stunning and dramatic 4x4 route that winds down the side of the Negev Plateau and ends in the Arava Valley, joining the main road to return by evening to Eilat.
Price per person: US$ 99 (Lunch included)
The tour will operate with a minimum of 5 participants.
The tour will depart from the Dan Hotel Lobby.
