El Al Israel Airlines

El Al Israel Airlines is the official airline of ECCB 2006 and is offering reduced fares for travel to the conference.
If traveling from USA, mention the fare code BIO103 when contacting El Al.
If traveling from elsewhere, mention the fare code G/S21/CSE/P2 when contacting El Al.
Please see the letter from El Al for further details.
Visas for visiting Israel

Participants from most countries do not need a visa to enter Israel. Please see The Researcher's Mobility Portal - Israel for an up-to-date list of countries from which visas are not required. You should be sure that your passport is valid for at least six months from the time that you enter Israel and that you have a return ticket for leaving Israel.

Please contact the Secretariat if you need a letter of invitation for obtaining a visa.
