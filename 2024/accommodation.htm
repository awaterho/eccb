Accommodation

Staying in Turku

We have selected Turku city centre hotels to recommend for you. All hotels have special room rates for the ECCB 2024 Congress participants.

Please make your booking directly to the hotel, see contact details below. The hotel fees include breakfast, service and VAT. All hotel fees are payable directly to the hotel.

Please note that the rooms can only be booked according to the hotels booking situation and we strongly encourage you to make the hotel reservations well in advance as there are many events happening in Turku around the time of the congress.

We hope you will enjoy your stay in Turku!

Scandic hotel Hamburger Börs

Eerikinkatu 4, 20100 Turku
+358 2 4785 1700
hamburgerbors@scandichotels.com

Read more

Iconic Hamburger Börs has been hosting guests since 1894 and enjoys a top location, right in the centre of Turku by the market square. The completely new and modern Hamburger Börs opened in November 2021. This elegantly refurbished hotel provides unforgettable hotel experiences in a modern setting and peaceful accommodation in the hotel’s new annex, where stylishly designed rooms ensure a great night’s sleep. Some rooms overlook the market square whilst others face our quiet courtyard.

Hamburger Börs hotel is easy to reach and everything is located close by. Its central location right next to the market square and near the Aurajoki river ensures good connections, and shopping streets, restaurants, cafes and sights are within easy reach. The hotel has its own garage and there is also a large public garage close by.

After a fruitful congress day sit back and relax with a drink in Börs rooftop bar and just enjoy beautiful views from the terrace on the 9th floor.

Standard, single room 164 eur/night
Standard, double room 184 eur/night

Reservations can be made using the booking code BECC010924 on the website, by phone or email to the hotel, using the hotel contact details above.

Please note that the reservations in Scandic hotel Hamburger Börs must be made by 1 September 2024 or on the basis of hotel’s booking situation.

Hotel Scandic Plaza Turku

Yliopistonkatu 29, 20100 Turku
+358 2 33200
plaza.turku@scandichotels.com

Read more

Scandic Plaza Turku is a comfortable hotel in the centre of Turku, along a pedestrian street. Hotel building is protected – a true pearl of 1920s functionalism. The new Turku Market Square, the banks of the Aurajoki River and the other sights of Turku are within walking distance or otherwise easily reachable.

The hotel interior has a calm and harmonious style. Enjoy tasty food for breakfast, lunch or dinner in our restaurant. Individual aircooling in the rooms guarantees an enjoyable stay even in the heat of the summer.

If you feel like doing some shopping, the Hansa shopping centre is located across from the hotel. You can exercise in hotel gym. In the summer you can borrow a bicycle from the hotel reception. Parking is conveniently available in the paid parking garage next door.

Hotel Scandic Plaza is certified under the Nordic Swan Ecolabel.

Original Sokos hotel Wiklund is an ideal choice for travellers who appreciate central location. A completely renewed urban hotel is located on the corner of the market square. Everything is close at hands restaurants, shopping streets and the new lively Turku market square.

A variety of restaurants and the roof top panoramic bar Walo offer their services for visitors. And of course everything else you would expect: Free breakfast, free Wi-Fi, quality and comfort from lovely snug beds. When staying in Wiklund, you will have also full access to the hotel sauna Wihta.

You check in at the reception, which also happens to be the hotel’s trendy coffee bar lounge.

Eerikinkatu 23, 20100 Turku

Solo Sokos Hotel Turku Seurahuone is located in the centre of Turku, a few blocks from the Market Square. When staying in Hotel Seurahuone you can enjoy the hotel breakfast prepared with artisan products.

Hotel Seurahuone is an elegant and active boutique hotel, a classic full of surprises, brought from the 1920s to the modern age. Air-conditioning, luxurious beds and a minibar are featured in each room. Each room is unique in size and decor. Solo breakfast includes carefully selected local artisanal products, fresh coffee and first class service – the ingredients for a perfect breakfast made especially for you.

Hotel Seurahuone also offers a gym and sauna for hotel guests.

Sokos Hotel Seurahuone is eco-label awarded. Green Key is the largest global eco-label for accommodation.

Original Sokos Hotel Kupittaa

Joukahaisenkatu 6, 20520 Turku
+358 300 870 000
sokos.hotels@sok.fi

Read more

Original Sokos Hotel Kupittaa is a modern hotel located right in the heart of Kupittaa, a fast-growing area in Turku. The atmosphere at the hotel is relaxed, while stylish, and the hotel staff do their very best to ensure your comfort.

Enjoy a hearty breakfast or lunch, pop in for a drink after meeting at the rooftop bar Kuppis or spend a leisurely evening in the atmospheric Elli bistro.

Universities of Turku are located close by and the conference venue Joki only a stone’s throw away.

You can reach the centre of Turku on foot, by Fölläri city bike or local bus from right in front of the hotel. There is a direct connection to the hotel from the Kupittaa train station. The hotel also has parking.

Centro Hotel

Yliopistonkatu 12a, 20100 Turku
+358 2 211 8100
centro@centrohotels.com

Read more

Centro Hotel is a modern and warm family hotel located right in the heart of Turku city centre, but away from the hubbub of the city, in an inner courtyard. Hotel offers its guests personal service and peaceful nights in the cozy rooms with comfortable beds.

Hotel breakfast has gained recognition. At the Centro hotel you can enjoy breakfast prepared with fresh natural ingredients from the nearby area. Foreign guests appreciate traditional delicacies, such as Karelian pasties, Finnish squeaky cheese and cloudberry jam.

As a hotel guest, you have free access to a modern fitness centre Fitness Palatsi, located in the same block as the hotel. Hotel Centro has own parking area in the inner yard.
