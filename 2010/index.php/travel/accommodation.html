Accommodation

ATTENTION! Most of the options we have taken on the hotels for our participants expire mid-july. We cordially advise you to book your stay as soon as possible.

Participants are requested to make their own arrangements for accommodation. Please find below direct links to the hotels, bed and breakfasts, and youth hostels in Ghent. Participants are advised to book their accommodation early as Belgium will be chairing the European Union from July to December 2010 and several related events are planned across Belgium.

Hotels

See map with the location of several Ghent hotels.
