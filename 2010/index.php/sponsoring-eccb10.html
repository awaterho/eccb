Sponsoring ECCB10
Sponsoring

Life Sciences: a “MUST ATTEND” event…

ECCB10 will serve as the 9th Annual European Conference on Computational Biology.  It is one of the largest and most important computational biology events globally in 2010.  Part of the ECCB conference series, it will be organized this year jointly by the Belgian and Dutch bioinformatics communities.

Leading Bioinformatics researchers, Computational Biologists, and Bio-IT professionals from Europe and around the world gather annually at the European Conference on Computational Biology (ECCB) for four days of presentations, poster sessions, workshops, software demonstrations, and most importantly—networking. ECCB is the leading European meeting and a primary international conference at which people report on and discuss advances in this rapidly-expanding field.

This ECCB10 event is a significant opportunity for you and your organization to build visibility and collaborate with the leaders in the fast changing fields from all over the world.  It will provide unique moments to showcase your tools, technologies, products and services to researchers , academics and industry.

We expect over 600 participants to take part in this exciting annual event. We invite you to join us at ECCB10, to let people in this emerging field know of your offerings that can help them to work more productively.

Why you should sponsor

The ECCB10 Sponsorship Program provides strategic opportunities for companies and other organizations to make an impact at the ECCB10 Annual Conference. Sponsoring ECCB can help you maximize corporate visibility, reach leading scientists, showcase new products and services, and establish new strategic partners and clients.

The majority of our congress budget will be spent on: logistic venue, travel & lodging of our key note speakers, poster awards, catering and the social event. Therefore we appreciate all financial and in-kind support.
